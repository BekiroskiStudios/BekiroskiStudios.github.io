Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2024

Welcome to Aurora. Your privacy is our top priority, and we want to be completely transparent about our privacy practices.

Data Collection

Aurora is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. All image generation happens locally on your device, and your prompts and generated images are stored only on your device.

Local Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Your generated images
  • Your image prompts
  • App preferences

You have complete control over this data and can delete it at any time from within the app.

Image Generation

When you generate an image, your prompt is processed locally. The generated images are stored only on your device and can be deleted at any time.

Your Privacy Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, you maintain complete control over your information. You can:

  • Delete any generated images
  • Clear your prompt history
  • Reset app preferences
