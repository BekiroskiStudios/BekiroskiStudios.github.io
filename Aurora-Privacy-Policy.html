Welcome to Aurora. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our AI image generation application.

We collect the following types of information:

  • Error logs and performance data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for:

  • Generating AI images based on your prompts

4. Data Processing and Third-Party Services

We use third-party services for image generation and app functionality:

  • Image Generation API (Currently third-party API, transitioning to Stable Diffusion)

Note: Generated images are not stored on your device or our servers permanently. They are temporarily processed through our image generation API and then delivered to your device.
